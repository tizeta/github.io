Highland Shortbread 1.

      1 lb. flour | i lb. fresh butter
      
      4 oz. sifted sugar
      
      Work the flour and sugar into the butter, then halve the dough and knead it out into two rounds, nick round edges with thumb and prick with fork, divide the rounds in four, and bake in a moderate oven.
      
      Miss M. Black, Oronsay, Connell.
   
  
      From The Feill Cookery Book, publication date 1907
