Marmalade 1.

    12 Seville oranges I Juice of 2 large lemons

    4 sweet oranges I 6 quarts water
    
    12 lbs. white sugar
    
    Cut the oranges in thin slices and put them to soak in the water for 24 hours. Then boil in the same water for 2 hours ; boil the pips in a muslin bag with the rest, but take out before adding the sugar. Add sugar and boil another hour. Add the lemon juice, and boil now not more than another ^ hour. Will fill about 8 quart pots.
    
    Mrs. Berthon.
    
    From The Feill Cookery Book, publication date 1907
